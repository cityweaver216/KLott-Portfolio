Hello, Hello!

My Beginnings

I’m Kate from Cleveland, Ohio, avid urban self-sufficiency enthusiast and storyteller. I went to school for photography, urban studies, and theater, and made fan websites for all my favorite science fiction and fantasy shows. But as a career, I was a nonprofit manager and fundraiser, particularly in the area of food and hunger. I’ve organized food pantries, farmer’s markets, community gardens, and urban farms. But in the rush to prepare the day’s food, all hands were often needed in the kitchen and I became worryingly aware of how few kitchen skills I had. Fixing that led to a prep cook job, which sent me on a trajectory into Italian and Japanese fine dining and nutritional therapy dining at Cleveland Clinic.

Changing Careers

Six years in the kitchen life has left me without drive. I haven’t volunteered at a farmer’s market in years! And I’ve neglected my core mission: to be an indispensable manager for community-minded small businesses and nonprofits. Now, I’d like to return to the computer skills I left behind as a child, skills that are essential for the 21st century economy. I’m excited to join CareerFoundry and study e-commerce web development.

My Dream

Although, to be honest, I’m most excited to earn the income needed to build my dream urban homestead: a straw bale cottage on a city lot with garden space, 4 chickens, 3 rabbits, and a goat.
